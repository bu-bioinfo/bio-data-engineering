## Theme

FAIR principles & stewardship

## Concept segment

FAIR operationalized rather than recited. Metadata and catalogs (findability at institutional scale). Access protocols; interoperability via shared vocabularies (ties to week 4); reusability, licensing, provenance (ties forward to weeks 9–10).

## Studio build

Assess a real public biological resource against a FAIR rubric; identify concrete gaps; draft a prioritized remediation plan. Then assess the recurring dataset's own FAIRness as a mirror exercise.
